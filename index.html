I Am
RAKHI ROY HALDER

I am an Educationist,Writer and Social Scientist

Dr. Rakhi Roy Halder, Associate Professor of Loreto College, Kolkata is author, Life skill coach. She also expresses her thoughts through paintings. She has organized unique research programmes sponsored by Ministry of Culture (Government of India) and introduced the concept of 'Life Oriented Interdisciplinary Research’. She is the founder of Stress-Free Lifestyle Hub. got my way through extremely stressful situations in life by applying the quantum science concepts in my life. The breeding ground of this course is my life experiences. She has first experienced the effects of the concepts of quantum science in her life, then wrote three books Myth breaker, Mystic Love & Secrets of Creation that reveal the relation between the quantum world and the human self, and finally she moved forward to speak about the tested stress-free lifestyle gaining formula through her courses uploaded in Udemy. She spends most of her time in research-oriented studies related to 'Quantum Consciousness & Visible Reality.' She founded Stress-Free Lifestyle Hub to help Stressful Individuals, Business Executives, Students, and any individual suffering from stress due to low self-esteem, low productivity, concentration crisis, and life dissatisfaction. She enables them to gain peace of mind by the blended knowledge and tools of quantum science & eastern philosophy. The vision and mission of the Stress-free lifestyle hub founded by her are to establish an inner power-centric education system that can create a generation of productive & stress-free individuals. The vision behind all her efforts is to highlight the need of life oriented education, to create scientific consciousness about the 'Self’ through education, to bring life close to nature,to tune life with cosmic rhythm, to challenge fast life style as it confines the 'Self’ to the materialistic world and to find out scientific and sustainable ways to establish peace and harmony in world to make the world a better place to live in. She is the member of 'Religion for Peace’ (RFP), United Nations Plaza New York and Editorial Board Member of IJBST Group of Journal. Her present area of research is ’Universal Consciousness & Quantum Reality’
